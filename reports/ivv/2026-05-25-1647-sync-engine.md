## Discrepancies

- **A1 (found at PLAN gate, HIGH) — FIXED.** The old `pull` REPLACED local with remote, which could
  permanently wipe an unsynced local import (the only copy of parsed PDF data). Escalated to the
  architect → chose the merge/union (OneNote/Simplenote) model. Implemented in `src/lib/sync/merge.ts`;
  `pull` now unions. EXECUTE independently confirmed the no-silent-loss guard (pull AND sync).
- **A4 (PLAN gate, MEDIUM) — resolved by Designer.** "Valid length" pinned to AES payload ≥ 28 bytes;
  decode-side guard; contract updated.
- **Merged `version` semantics (EXECUTE, minor) — resolved by Designer.** Contract R-M4 added: a merge
  never downgrades the schema version (`max(a, b, STORE_VERSION)`). Safe; moot in v1.
- **A2 (LOW, pre-authorized) — observed:** `sync()` does a single pull+retry; a second consecutive
  conflict propagates (no unbounded loop). Consistent with the spec's singular "re-attempt."
- No other discrepancies.
